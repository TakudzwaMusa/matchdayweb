Benefits

Discover how everyone wins on Matchday Superfan

Clubs

Rewards

Clubs on Matchday Superfan unlock new ways to generate revenue. By creating matches and having dedicated channel hosts, they can sell more tickets and engage fans beyond the stadium. Every game hosted opens an opportunity for clubs to earn money from fan engagement, strengthening their brand and building stronger communities.

Fandom

Rewards

Fandoms bring fans together in one place. Channel hosts create experiences, discussions, and exclusive content that fans subscribe to. The more a fandom grows, the more the hosts benefit financially. This ensures that fan passion directly rewards creators who build engaging communities.

Superfan

Rewards

Superfans are at the heart of the platform. By engaging in fandoms and channels, they earn points that can be redeemed for real rewards. Superfans also unlock exclusive sponsor discounts on merchandise and products. Their loyalty and activity translates directly into value they can use in the real world.

Sponsor

Rewards

Sponsors gain visibility and conversions by marketing products directly to engaged fans. By putting their products on the app, they connect with highly targeted audiences. Sponsors also play a key role in funding leaderboards and challenges, ensuring fans and superfans have more incentives to participate. This creates a win-win for both brand growth and fan satisfaction.
