App Info

Everything you need to know about using Matchday Superfan

App Overview

Matchday Superfan is a social media platform designed to bring home-viewing fans (SuperFans) as close as possible to the real in-stadium experience. Whether you’re cheering from your couch or from the stands, the app makes sure you feel connected, engaged, and part of the action.

For SuperFans at home, it’s about contributing to your team’s journey from anywhere in the world, while still enjoying the thrill and energy of the stadium. For fans attending live matches, it’s about enhancing that stadium experience with premium content features – creating a virtual stadium that others can join.

How It Works (App Model)

  • Stadium-attending fans can scan their matchday tickets to create and host a Channel called “Fandom.” Think of it like being the admin of a chat group – only you can start the channel, but anyone can join.
  • Each host can create only one channel per match, while home fans can follow unlimited channels.
  • Matches will only appear on the app once a match has been created by the Event Host (Club/Event organizer).
  • Multiple sporting codes are supported. Fans only receive notifications from the channels they follow.

User Categories

  • Fandom Hosts (Stadium-attending Fans): Verified ticket holders who attend the match and create fandom channels.
  • SuperFans: Fans who subscribe for premium features, exclusive content, and a closer connection with their favorite teams and fellow fans.
  • Clubs/Teams: Official representatives who verify their status to participate.
  • Commercial Partners (Sponsors): Verified partners who use the platform for brand engagement, sponsorships, and fan rewards.
  • ExPros (Ex-Professionals): Verified former players who provide experience, authenticity, and insights.

Leaderboards & Rewards

Fans earn points through their engagement and passion display for their team by posting, commenting, voting, and participating in activities such as Match Ups (Line Ups and Tactical comparisons), Quizzes, know-your-player, and many other challenges. Fans are offered more chances of winning through topic-based leader boards called FanCharts (Food, Fashion, Pets, etc.) as well as on regional leaderboards by City and Country. SuperFans also unlock exclusive rewards, sponsor discounts, and redemption opportunities.

Business Model

The app is based on a freemium model. Free users can enjoy standard, yet exciting features such Fandom participation which includes; posting, tactical insights, passion ratings, player Match Ups, quizzes, and many other fan challenges.

Premium users (SuperFans) get access to exclusive features like Match Ups, Fan Videos (Matchday memorabilia, Fan-brand enhancing videos, Birthday value videos, and many more), PowerPlay participation, Celebration contests, Face Swaps challenges, and additional perks.

Clubs, hosts, and sponsors also participate through ticket verification, and brand promotions. Built-in payment gateways handle both subscriptions and rewards for smooth transactions.

How to Get Started

Getting started is simple! Choose your sports category and check in as one of the following user types:

  • Channel Host: Select the match you will host, purchase a ticket, host your Fandom, and attend the game at your stadium. Hosts are responsible for creating Fandoms/Channels.
  • SuperFan: Subscribe to support your favorite team and unlock premium access and exclusive content on Fandom and SuperFan channels.
  • Club/Team: Verify your official status to participate in the platform and engage your fanbase.
  • Commercial Partner (Sponsor): Verify your business or sponsorship credentials to engage with fans, provide rewards, and promote your brand.

Once signed in, you can join channels, participate in challenges, create content, and start earning points. SuperFans gain access to premium features, while Hosts, Clubs, and Sponsors can actively manage their Fandoms and engage fans respectively.
