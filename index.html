Intro

Matchday Technologies has developed the Matchday SuperFan App which is designed to promote stadium attendances by enhancing the fan match day experience and fundraising activities. The SuperFan App is rewards based, and operated on a ‘Freemium’ business model and therefore, once fully established; its revenues will contribute significantly towards the Sports infrastructure, grassroots sports development, and ultimately boost the economy of the country.

This revolutionary App is an all encompassing tool which enhances the user’s match day experience through exciting features and provides the user with a user-profile and memorabilia with each event attended. The Matchday SuperFan App is a platform which enables stadium-attending fans (Fandom Hosts) to host fellow sporting loving fans (SuperFans from across the globe) on Channels called Fandoms. The fans are ranked, rated, and profiled according various leader boards based on their performances compared to other SuperFans including the performances of their hosts.

The App profiles its users through passion ratings using the following criteria:

  • Who is the Town/City/Country/World’s most passionate SuperFan?
  • What is the Town/City/Country/World’s most loved sport?
  • The most passionate Nation in the world?
  • The most passionate Town/City in the world?
  • The most passionate Team/Club in the world?
  • The world’s most loved sporting personality of all time?
  • The biggest sporting entertainers?
  • The most stylish SuperFans + Fashion Designers?
  • The best connoisseurs + Chefs?
  • The world’s most passionate Super Pets?
  • The world’s most sport loving families?
